# Shoutouts and Acknowledgements

This document is designed to acknowledge all registeries and repositories where Vulnforge takes its inspiration, and sometimes even code, from. It is also a place to give shoutouts to the people who have helped make Vulnforge possible. (and also help me remember which repos I need to clone lmao)

## Repositories

- [VulnHub](https://www.vulnhub.com/) - The original inspiration for Vulnforge, and the source of many of the vulnerable services used in the templates.
- [HackTheBox](https://www.hackthebox.eu/) - My first exposure to CTFs and the reason I love it so dang much.
- [VulHub](https://github.com/vulhub/vulhub) - A collection of pre-configured vulnerable Docker environments for security testing. The first large repository, and the current one for testing.
